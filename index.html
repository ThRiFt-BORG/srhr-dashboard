SRHR Advocacy Data Portal

Project Period: Counties Covered: 4 Last Updated:

This portal tracks the Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) advocacy landscape across four target counties in Kenya — Homa Bay, Migori, Kilifi, and Kwale — as part of EACHRights' three-year project: "Taking on the Legal and Policy Advocacy Challenge: Promoting Synergies Among SRHR Social Movements in Kenya," funded by Amplify Change. The dashboard monitors policy implementation status, advocacy priorities, CSO activities, and budget trends to support evidence-based advocacy and coordination.

About This Portal

This portal is maintained by EACHRights (East African Centre for Human Rights) with support from Amplify Change. Data is updated by project field teams. To add new advocacy updates, open the county page and use the "Add Update" panel at the bottom.

For questions or data corrections, contact the EACHRights monitoring and evaluation team. Source documents are available on individual county pages.
